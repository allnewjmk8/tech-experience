A bit about me.

Hello! Welcome to my personal website I made during Girls Who Code's accelerated Summer Immersion Program. This is an exciting opportunity for me to be able to share my website! Computer science and technology has been an interest of mine for several years, so I'm always looking for new experiences to expand my horizons.

During school, I participate in CS classes and outside of class I'm on FRC robotics team #5572. When I'm not staying late after school, I enjoy yoga, creative writing, photography, and art. Volunteering is another big part of my life as well. I like to support organizations that support me, so I often volunteer at robotics competitions.
